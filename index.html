⬇ Download APK
To install this app on Android:
  1. Click Download APK to download the file.
  2. Go to Settings → Security → Unknown Sources and enable the toggle.
  3. Open the downloaded APK from your file manager and tap Install.
  4. Once installed, open the app and enjoy!
⚠️ You must enable "Unknown Sources" in Settings to install apps outside the Play Store.
